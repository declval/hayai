Placing Fingers

Place your fingertips on the home row (the middle row of the keyboard) keys highlighted below. You can easily find them by the fingerprint icons on top of them. Your thumbs should rest on the space bar.

Pressing keys

Press keys according to the colors below. For example use your left index finger to press the keys 4, 5, R, T, F, G, V, B, your left middle finger to press 3, E, D, C and so on... After each key press return the finger back to the home row.

Settings

If you want to leave the color guide on while practicing enable it in settings. There you can also add a text of your choice, choose how many characters to display per screen and more.
